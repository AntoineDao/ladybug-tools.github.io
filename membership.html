Become a patron

We have been working on the development of Ladybug Tools since 2013, and with the help from some awesome people from around the globe Ladybug Tools not only has “survived” but has flourished during the last couple of years. It’s now a industry standard toolkit, with more than 100,000 downloads only for Grasshopper and is used by professionals and is taught in architecture and engineering schools. To create a lasting support we started a Patreon page to collect recurrent contributions. You can read the full story about why, how and what and become a supporter by checking the Patreon page.

Become an industry partner

We understand the vital importance in creating a partnership with the industry in order to further apply Ladybug Tools in environmental building design. We have created various options for fostering this relationship with the goal of creating a mutually beneficial partnership allowing for continued support of Ladybug Tools.

Because of the limited number of hours that we have available, the number of industry partners for each year is limited and will be on the basis of first come first serve.

Bronze

$5,000/year

Only 4 will be available each year

It’s an annual commitment which will be paid on a quarterly basis. You’ll have:

  • Early access to the latest videos
  • 1 hour Bronze partners monthly calls. This call will be shared with other Bronze parteners. The call is for whatever topics you would like to discuss, including but not limited to:
    • Answering questions related to your projects
    • Discussing limitations of industry-standard methods
    • Best practices
    • Propose new tutorial videos
  • Your logo will be on the main page as a Bronze industry partner

Become an institutional partner

We are so proud that Ladybug Tools is helpful for educating the next generation of architects and engineers. Institutional partnership provides a solution for educational institutions to support Ladybug Tools. There are two ways to become Institutional partner, you can contribute $10 per student or a suggested amount of $750 to Ladybug Tools on an annualy basis. In return we will

- give all your students early access to the latest on-demand videos for free.
- review your teaching materials related to Ladybug Tools.
- put your logo on the home page and in our presentation slides as an institutional partner.
